About Me

I am a self-taught front-end developer with a strong working knowledge of HTML, CSS, and Javascript. This has been the foundation of my learning as I have learnt, and am currently improving my knowledge on, React (which you can see below), as well as other toolsets such as Figma.

Always looking for oppurtunities to learn and include new methods and functions in my code, I pride my work in being made clearly and concisely with a style that suits the project at hand.

Whether it be a freelance project for a client, or a web app for my portfolio, I always take the time to make them fully responsive - which has now become an area of web developement in which I personally feel I excell in.

When I'm not working on my craft I spent my time playing sport, reading, seeing live music, researching how to improve my desk posture, and watching films.
